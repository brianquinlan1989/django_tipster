How The Game Works

  1. Entry fee is €20.00.
  2. Pick one horse per race each day.
  3. Person with the most amount of points at the end of Day 4 is the winner.
  4. Prize money and number of winners will depend on number of entries.
  5. Points are awarded on the return starting price odds of the horse. (eg. starting price odds @ 4/1 returns you 4 points)
  6. If your horse places, you receive ¼ of the points regardless of the type of race.
  7. The number of places in a race and odds will be determined by PaddyPower website.
  8. Selections must be made before 13.30 each day or entry will be invalid. You can not edit your selections after 13.30 for any race.
  9. If your selection happens to become a non-runner, you will be giving horse number 1 in the race.
  10. Leaderboard will be updated each evening with points total.
  11. In the unlikely event of a tie, the winner will be determined by the number of winners, and then the number of places if needs be.
