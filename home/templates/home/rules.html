How The Game Works

The Boring Stuff

  • Entry fee is €25.00

  • Pick one horse per race each day

  • Make your selections for Day 1 before 13.30 on Tuesday. Follow suit for the other 3 days

  • You can edit your selections as many times upto the deadline

  • The person with the most points at the end of Day 4 is the winner

  • Points are awarded on the return starting price odds of the horse. (eg. starting price odds @ 4/1 returns you 4 points)

  • If your horse places, you receive ¼ of the points regardless of the type of race

  • The number of places in a race will be determined by PaddyPower website

  • The return starting price will be determined by SportingLife website

  • Prize money and number of winners will depend on number of entries

  • Selections must be made before 13.30 each day or entry will be invalid. You can not edit your selections after 13.30 for any race.

  • If you do not make selections for a particular day, no points will be awarded.

  • If your selection happens to become a non-runner after the 13.30 deadline, you will be giving horse number 1 in the race.

  • Leaderboard will be updated after each race.

  • In the unlikely event of a tie, the winner will be determined by the number of winners, and then the number of places if needs be.
